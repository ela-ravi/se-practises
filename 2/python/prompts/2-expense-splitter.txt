
Role: You are an experienced python developer in streamlit.
Context: A bunch of friends go out for a dinner / trip. They need an expense splitter to split the amounts equally.
Task: 
- Develop a streamlit app for expense splitter.
- Take following inputs from user
     1. Total Amount
     2. No of friends
     3. Name (optional) - no of this fields should be updated based on No of friends dynamically
     4. Contribution (Optional) - no of this fields should be updated based on No of friends dynamically
- Calculate and split the expenses equally and show Who owes/gets back howmuch
Example:
Input:
  - No of friends = 3
  - Total Amount = 9000
  - Name1 = F1, contribution = 0
  - Name2 = F2, Contribution = 3500
  - Name3 = F3, Contribution = 5500
Output:
  - F1 -> owes -> 500 to F2 and 2500 to F3
Tone:
A nice simple UI.
